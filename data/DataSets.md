### AmazonHistoryPrice
- This dataset contains 930 products and their real prices sourced from camelcamelcamel across 18 product categories.
- Each productâ€™s data includes the product name, description, features, lowest and highest price, current price, list price, and an image link.
- This dataset was sourced from the "Measuring Bargaining Abilities of LLMs: A Benchmark and A Buyer-Enhancement Method" publication that can be found at https://aclanthology.org/2024.findings-acl.213.pdf
- The dataset is contained in the publications repository at https://github.com/TianXiaSJTU/AmazonPriceHistory
- Published by: Tian Xia, Zhiwei He, Tong Ren, Yibo Miao, Zhuosheng Zhang, Yang Yang*, Rui Wang*

### CaSiNo: A Corpus of Campsite Negotiation Dialogues for Automatic Negotiation Systems
- This dataset contains dialogue of 1030 negotiations where two participants take the roles of campsite neighbors and negotiat for resources
- Referenced in "CaSiNo: A Corpus of Campsite Negotiation Dialogues for Automatic Negotiation Systems" by Kushal Chawla, Jaysa Ramirez, Rene Clever, Gale Lucas, Jonathan May, Jonathan Gratch
- Published by: Kristina Toutanova, Anna Rumshisky, Luke Zettlemoyer, Dilek Hakkani-Tur, Iz Beltagy, Steven Bethard, Ryan Cotterell, Tanmoy Chakraborty, Yichao Zhou
- This dataset was sourced from https://aclanthology.org/2021.naacl-main.254/ and downloaded using convoKit at https://convokit.cornell.edu/documentation/casino-corpus.html#:~:text=Data%20License-,CaSiNo%20Corpus,Association%20for%20Computational%20Linguistics.
- License: Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) license (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/)

### Deal or No Deal Game Data
- This dataset contains decisions made by contestants in the Deal or No Deal gameshow, Netherlands (51), Germany (47), and the United States (53)
- Referenced in "Deal or No Deal? Decision Making under Risk in a Large-Payoff Game Show" by Thierry Post, Martijn J. van den Assem, Guido Baltussen and Richard H. Thaler. Found at https://www.anderson.ucla.edu/documents/areas/fac/accounting/Class%201%20-%20Thaler%20-%20Deal%20or%20No%20Deal.pdf
- This dataset was sourced from https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/jaredstock/deal-or-no-deal-game-data and https://www.openicpsr.org/openicpsr/project/113232/version/V1/view;jsessionid=A9BF239BFD4C286A090FA82FA2EC1113
- License: Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International Public License (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/)

### StanfordNLP - Cragslist Bargains
- This dataset contains dialogues between two agents, a buyer and a seller, that negotiat over the price of an item for sale
- Referenced in "Decoupling Strategy and Generation in Negotiation Dialogues" by He He and Derek Chen and Anusha Balakrishnan and Percy Liang. Found at https://arxiv.org/pdf/1808.09637
- It can be found at https://huggingface.co/datasets/stanfordnlp/craigslist_bargains
- License: Unknown
